The TrashPack (and TrashPack 2)

The TrashPack was an old Minecraft 1.8.9 resource pack made by xad1561 specifically designed to be as terrible to use as humanly possible. It contains many memes from the 2022-2023 era, but was never truly finished. It has been left mostly in the state it was when last updated.

You can download it here


A sequel of the TrashPack for newer Minecraft versions is currently being made. Check back here soon to see when it releases.
