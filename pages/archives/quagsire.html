Quagsire

WARNING: COMPETITIVE POKÉMON AHEAD. PROCEED IF YOU DARE.

At first glance, Quagsire doesn't seem like a very important Pokémon. However, Quagsire is actually far stronger than its 435 base stat total implies. Quagsire has the ability Unaware, which lets it ignore stat changes on other Pokémon. This makes it able to counter stat-boosted Pokémon that could otherwise sweep an entire team without issue.

Because of this, Quagsire has kind of a reputation for being able to walk into the highest tier of a given generation, counter the most oppressive 'mon in the entire tier, and then do precisely nothing else. So what would happen if we brought 6 of them onto a team?

In Generation 8 Anything Goes, the Quagsire team was able to win a few times but didn't work particularly well as Quagsire's Water/Ground typing leaves it extremely vulnerable to Grass moves and Freeze Dry. In Generation 9 however, we got Tera Types, which lets you change the typing of one Pokémon in a battle. Now with the ability to remove its one singular weakness, Quagsire was now free to run in around in AG and slay all that dared to stand before it.

Much rage ensued from Pokémon Showdown tryhards that lost to a random guy with 6 Quagsires while they were using useless terrible Pokémon like Miraidon or Calyrex-Shadow.
xad1561 has run the 6 Quagsire team in Pokémon Showdown many times before. The only format where this team is valid is Anything Goes, so it needs to be able to account for the fact that literally anything can show up. Much refinement of this team in battles means that it has a lot of seemingly random choices that are meant to counter specific Pokémon.

Some examples of seemingly random but actually very important decisions include:

  • Earth Power to prevent suicide lead Glimmora from spreading Toxic Spikes
  • Water Absorb on one Quagsire to stop Draco Meteor Miraidon from sweeping the team, because yes this is one of the few cases where Unaware is actually a detriment and yes this caused the team to get swept once
  • One Quagsire with Tera-Dark+Tera Blast to kill Calyrex-Shadow, and two more that have Tera-Normal to stop it if that fails
  • Covert Cloak to stop Garganacl from killing the entire team, and Waterfall over Liquidation to have 8 more PP to be able to PP stall it more effectively
  • Focus Sash and Counter on two Quagsire, and Tera-Fire+Tera Blast on another to have a fighting chance against Grass-types
  • Every Quagsire having no nickname and being set to always be the same gender so that it's more difficult to tell the difference between each Quagsire from the opponent's perspective (yep this team was minmaxed that much)
