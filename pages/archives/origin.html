Origin of The Sink

The name of The Sink originated from a small edit war over the name of a group chat between the four original members: Semaj7373, xad1561, Uranium215, and galaxolotl (grunt)




This edit war ended when Semaj renamed the group chat to the sink, and the name has stuck from then on

In early 2025, the Republic of The Sink was made originally as a small Discord server, but since has gained a website in 2026 as you can probably tell.
